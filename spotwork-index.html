Spotwork

The app designed for people seeking flexible work and instant pay.

Overview:

  • Site name: Spotwork

  • Description: The app designed for people seeking flexible work and instant pay.

  • My Role: 1 of 4 UX/UI Designers, UX Researchers

  • Duration: March 2023 - April 2023

  • Tools: Figma, Miro, Discord, Slack, Zoom

  • Stakeholders: UX Design Lead, Product Manager, Head of Business Development

Goal

In order to expand their customer base and broaden their market, Spotwork asked our group to build a high-fidelity prototype based on their current website, which is not yet perfect, to demonstrate the functionality of their product for the purpose of onboarding potential employers to their platform.

01 - Background

To build a standout product, it is essential to understand the competitive landscape and identify industry best practices as well as where our product falls within that landscape. We conducted competitive analysis against Spotwork’s top competitors, including not but limited to: Wonolo, UpShift, and Thumbtack to understand how others tackled problems that may have arose during our design process.

We collaborated with a diverse and cross-functional team to get up to speed with Spotwork’s current design language. This involved familiarizing ourselves with Spotwork's brand identity, by immersing ourselves in all their platforms from employer, employee, and developer perspectives via special access to their developer tools. Additionally, it’s important that we understood the structure and use cases of their visual elements by repeated use and reconstruction of fully functional pages and components. By aligning with the established design language, we ensured consistency across the platform, reinforcing Spotwork's brand and making the demo an authentic representation of the live service.

The fruition of our research and collaboration efforts allowed us to lay a strong foundation for the development of a demo that effectively highlights Spotwork's competitive edge in the crowded on-demand staffing market.

02 - User Testing

As a crucial part of the project, our team conducted comprehensive user testing to identify areas for improvement in the demo. This process involved in-person interviews and applying UX testing methodologies, such as usability testing and task analysis, to gather valuable insights into user behavior and preferences. Following the tests, we compiled our findings into an affinity map to discern patterns, pain points, and areas requiring further refinement.

In summary, two key findings emerged from the user testing: participants experienced difficulties completing the "Create Position" and "Post a Shift'' user flows. To address these issues, we proposed increasing the weight and visibility of the Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons for these flows, making it more intuitive for users to navigate and complete the tasks. By implementing these changes, we aimed to enhance the user experience and ensure the demo accurately represents the efficiency and ease-of-use that Spotwork's live service offers to its clients.

03 - Analysis

To ensure our demo effectively showcases the key features and functions of Spotwork's live service, we decided to map out the entire platform in its current state. With information architecture laid out and easily interpretable, we were able to holistically create a comprehensive site map and detailed maps of core user flows.

These maps served as essential tools for our team in multiple ways. First, they helped us identify the most important aspects of the live service to be highlighted in the demo, ensuring a comprehensive representation of Spotwork's capabilities. Second, the maps enabled us to detect any potential bottlenecks or redundancies in the existing user flows, allowing us to optimize the demo's design for an enhanced user experience.

In addition to the immediate benefits for the demo, these maps will also prove invaluable for the future development of the Spotwork app. They provide a solid foundation for iterating on the platform's fundamental design, enabling the team to make informed decisions when implementing new features, optimizing existing flows, or refining the platform's overall structure. By meticulously mapping the platform, our team has not only ensured a successful demo but also laid the groundwork for Spotwork's continued growth and innovation.

04 - Prototyping

With valuable insights from user testing in hand, our team moved forward to create a high fidelity prototype that accurately represents the look and feel of the final product. To work efficiently and maximize our collective skills, we coordinated the division of labor by assigning specific tasks to team members.

The interactive prototype can be found here

Effective communication played a crucial role in our success, ensuring that we stayed on the same page throughout the process. We established regular check-ins, progress updates, and feedback sessions to maintain a cohesive and collaborative environment. This approach enabled us to address any challenges promptly, iterate on our designs, and refine the user experience.

Figma served as our primary platform for building the high fidelity prototype, providing a collaborative and easy-to-use interface that facilitated seamless teamwork. By harnessing the power of Figma, we were able to create, iterate, and share our designs effortlessly, leading to a final prototype that showcases the full potential of Spotwork's live service for employers.

Final Thoughts

Completing my industry design placement project at Spotwork has been a truly rewarding experience. Working alongside a team of three other interns, we were tasked with researching, mapping, designing, and prototyping a functional demo for use during employer onboarding. Through this project, I gained valuable insights into the UX design process and reaffirmed the importance of constant communication, collaboration, and consistency. Communicating effectively with my team members and stakeholders was critical to ensure we were all working towards the same goals and delivering the best results possible. Collaborating with my team members allowed us to leverage diverse perspectives and generate innovative ideas that led to the success of the project. Finally, maintaining consistency throughout the design process ensured that all the design elements fit together seamlessly and created a cohesive experience.

Overall, my industry design placement project with Spotwork provided me with a valuable opportunity to apply my UX design skills to a real-world project and gain experience in a professional setting. I am grateful for the skills and knowledge I have gained during this placement, and I look forward to utilizing them in my future endeavors.
